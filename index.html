Something else about why people use it.

In our society, it's a startling reality that many women do not possess the depth of understanding about their own bodies as they should. This lack of awareness can lead to a disconnect from your own body, and can be unnecessarily stressful and anxiety inducing. We firmly believe that every woman deserves comprehensive, accessible information about her body, in order to cultivate a relationship of understanding, respect, and empowerment.
